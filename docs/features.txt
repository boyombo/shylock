name of application: shylock

kind of application: inventory management and point of sales

features:
easy to use
web-based - you can either set it up on your local computer or have it running on a website
data backup

modules:
stock - manages your inventory includes re-order level alert
suppliers/customers - sales by customer, 
receptions - track items when they come in
sales - optional barcode module for sales with barcode scanners
expenses

reports:
sales/expense trends (daily, weekly, monthly trends)
sales/expense breakdown by categories
profit & loss account

optional:
interface with custom accounting module (additional cost) to generate other accounting reports
